How to Use

Drag the background to rotate the apparatus. Use the slider or drag the rheostat in the 3D model to change current. Click the switch to start, click the battery to reverse polarity, and click the magnet to flip poles.

Physics: F = I × B . L (Lorentz Force). The magnet experiences an equal and opposite reaction force, which changes the scale reading via Newton's Third Law.

Suggested Activities
