Thermometer of Fate

You are trapped in a laboratory. To escape, you must heat a thermometer to exactly 60°C without exceeding it.

In the room, you find a sink, an electric kettle and a freezer.

Use the inputs below to mix boiling water (at 100°C) and ice (at -5°C) to achieve this precise temperature.

Specific Heat Capacities:

\( c_{\text{water}} = 4.18 \, \text{J/g} \cdot \text{°C} \)

\( c_{\text{ice}} = 2.1 \, \text{J/g} \cdot \text{°C} \)

Specific Latent Heats:

\( L_f = 334 \, \text{J/g} \) (Fusion of Ice)

\( L_v = 2260 \, \text{J/g} \) (Vaporization of Water)
